# 🔍 FocusLens — Screen Activity Dashboard

> **One score. Full clarity. Your focus, quantified.**

FocusLens is a privacy-first, browser-based dashboard that helps you track your screen time across Study, Social Media, and Entertainment. It turns your daily activities into a single **Focus Score (0–100)** and provides actionable insights to help you build better habits.



---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 **Real-Time Dashboard**
- **Focus Score** — A weighted metric that rewards study time and penalizes distractions
- **Category Breakdown** — See exactly how your time is distributed
- **Distraction Meter** — Track your Social + Entertainment percentage (target: <30%)
- **Hourly Heatmap** — Identify your peak focus hours and distraction windows

### 📝 **Activity Logging**
- Log activities by category (Study, Social Media, Entertainment)
- Track minutes spent and hour of day
- Data persists locally in your browser — no servers, no accounts

### 🔥 **Progress Tracking**
- **14-Day Focus Streak** — See your consistency over time
- **Weekly Overview** — Stacked bar chart showing your last 7 days
- **Historical Trends** — Compare day-to-day progress

### 🎨 **Themes**
- Dark Orange (Default)
- Light Purple
- Pastel Pink
- Matte Red

### 🔒 **Full Transparency**
- All calculations are deterministic — same inputs = same output
- No data leaves your browser
- Open formulas documented in-app
- No ads. No tracking. Ever.
